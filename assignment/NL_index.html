About NL Badminton Center

NL badminton Center, the word of ‘NL’ means New Life. Enterprise founder Yap Jyen Ming hope that people who comes to NL Badminton Center for playing badminton will get a healthy life. NL Badminton Center had decide 2 profesional badminton coach for badminton training program to find out more talents. Next, the courts in hall are good in system ventilation. NL Badminton Center is also got factory to produce their own brand badminton racquet and sportswear.

History of NL Badminton Center

NL Badminton Center (New Life Badminton Center ) since 1 March 2009, enterprise founder Yap Jyen Ming and shareholders which are Chin You Qi, Lee Jia Xin and also Loo Qing Xuan had decided to operate a badminton center. They decided to operate NL Badminton Center in Selangor to gather all the badminton lover. They learn more badminton knowledge with a fully badminton player Ooi Wei Ming. Mr Ooi had fully support them on the road to operate a badminton center. Since 1 January 2010, NL Badminton Center does business officially to today.
