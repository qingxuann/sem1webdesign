2020 Exclusive: FIREFLARE 500 limited edition design revealed

The popular FIREFLARE 500 is scheduled for a one-time production, limited edition design exclusive for 2020. Inspired by Japanese traditional artistry, the racquet takes visual and philosophical cues from “kinpaku,” which are thin sheets of gold often used to express delicateness, festivity, extravagance, and more. This limited edition FIREFLARE 500 will see a global release date of August 10th.


The FIREFLARE 500 is engineered for intermediate to advanced players looking to command the rally with maximum speed and effortless force. The AERO Frame built into the FIREFLARE 500 is developed to reduce air resistance for speed and acceleration. The careful combination with the ULTRA SONIC SYSTEM helps increase repulsion while softening the impact on the arm, providing increased shuttle speeds with an effortless swing.
